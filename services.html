What We Do

At Motley Corp, we offer a comprehensive suite of construction services to take your project from start to finish. Whether you need demolition, site preparation, or a full-scale build, we’re your trusted partner.

Site Development

Before you can build, your site needs to be properly prepared. We ensure your land is safe, level, and ready for construction.

  • Land Clearing: Removing trees, debris, and obstacles.
  • Excavation & Grading: Leveling and shaping the ground for proper drainage and stability.
  • Foundation Preparation: Laying the groundwork for a strong, long-lasting structure.
Demolition, Recycling, & Hauling

Whether you're removing an old structure or making room for something new, we provide safe, efficient demolition and debris hauling.

  • Structural Demolition: Complete teardown of buildings, warehouses, and commercial spaces.
  • Selective Demolition: Removing specific parts of a structure while keeping the rest intact.
  • Debris Removal, Hauling, & Recycling: Safe transportation and disposal of waste materials.
Abatement Services

We specialize in removing hazardous materials to keep construction sites safe and compliant with regulations.

  • Asbestos Removal: Safe extraction of asbestos-containing materials.
  • Lead Abatement: Eliminating lead-based paint and contaminated surfaces.
  • Mold Remediation: Identifying and removing mold hazards in commercial and industrial spaces.
Construction & Buildouts

From new commercial builds to renovations and tenant improvements, we handle every aspect of construction with precision.

  • New Commercial & Industrial Builds: Office buildings, warehouses, retail spaces, and more.
  • Renovations & Tenant Buildouts: Transforming existing spaces into modern, functional environments.
  • Custom Construction Solutions: Tailored approaches to meet your business's specific needs.

Our Process

STEP 1
Consultation & Planning

We start with a consultation to understand your goals, budget, and timeline. From there, we craft a strategic plan to bring your vision to life.

STEP 2
Site Preparation & Demolition

We clear the way—whether it's grading land, excavating, abating hazardous materials, or demolishing an existing structure.

STEP 3
Construction & Build-Outs

Once the site is ready, we begin construction, ensuring each phase is completed with precision and efficiency.

STEP 4
Quality Assurance & Final Touches

Before project completion, we conduct thorough inspections to ensure every detail meets our high standards.

STEP 5
Project Handover & Continued Support

We don’t just finish the job—we ensure you’re fully satisfied, providing ongoing support as needed.
