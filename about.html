Who We Are

Our Story

Contact Motley Corp today

Motley Corp was founded in 2021, but its foundation was laid long before that. Built on decades of experience in construction and demolition, the company is an evolution of Motley Construction Inc - a business shaped by hard work, craftsmanship, and a commitment to doing things the right way.

For brothers Courtney and Corey Motley, construction has always been more than just a trade - it's a family tradition. Their father was a serial entrepreneur, a leader in his field, and someone who believed that every project should be done with care, precision, and integrity. He set the standard for what it meant to build something that lasts not just in materials, but in reputation and trust.

When he passed, Courtney and Corey saw an opportunity to carry forward his legacy, not just by continuing the work he started, but by elevating it. Motley Corp was created to blend the values he instilled in them with modern innovation, bringing a new level of expertise and professionalism to every project.

Today, Motley Corp stands on the same principles that built their father's success: honesty, quality, and a commitment to leaving every project better than they found it. Because for them, construction is not only about the work. It's about the people, the relationships, and the trust that comes with doing the job right.

Our Mission

Motley Corp is built on grit, determination, and a commitment to doing things the right way - values we carry into every project we take on. At Motley Corp, we believe construction is more than just brick and steel; it's about creating opportunity, preserving legacies, and strengthening communities. We combine time-tested techniques with modern efficiency to ensure every project is completed with integrity, precision, and pride. From demolition to development, we're here to shape the future - one build at a time.
